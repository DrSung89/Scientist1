ğŸ Human Equivalent Dose (HED) Calculation

Translating drug doses from animal studies to human clinical trials requires normalization based on Body Surface Area (BSA), not just body weight. This is the FDA-recommended method for estimating the maximum safe starting dose.

The Conversion Formula

$$ HED (mg/kg) = Animal Dose (mg/kg) \times \frac{Animal K_m}{Human K_m} $$

Common Km factors: Mouse (3), Rat (6), Human (37). This calculator automates these conversions to ensure safety and accuracy in translational research.
